restyling a bad website

Suzanne Collins' Website

This assignment requried us to select a "badly designed" website and modify CSS elements to improve it. Some notable improvements I made was to adjust the color scheme to match some colors already present on the page. Additionally, I changed the font to make it more readable.

Two "controversial" decisions I made was to:

  • Color-out the notifications banner so that the website's "disclaimer" was removed. I felt that the presence of this disclaimer ruined the credibility of the design, and I used CSS to downplay its prominence.
  • I did not change much of the padding/margins. There WERE some changes made, but they are subtle. I feel that the columns on this site are anchored well, and shifting them left or right would have made the design less aesthetic.
